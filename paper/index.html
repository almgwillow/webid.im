Purpose
Instant Messaging over Linked Data and WebID.

Abstract

Realtime communications on the web have come to rely almost exclusively on data silos with communications going through trusted third parties. While this works well enough in most cases it suffers from the weakness that the user is not fully in control of their own data and not able to use it in highly connected scenarios to created unexpected reuse. e present a virtual wallet powered by Social Linked Data (SoLiD), in order to, better distribute data, protect privacy and allow next generation applications accessible by both human and machine.
